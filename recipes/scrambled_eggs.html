Scrambled Eggs

Description: Scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • One egg
  • 1/2 tablespoon of butter
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. place pan on stove with medium heat
  2. place butter into pan until melted and barely sizzling
  3. crack egg into pan
  4. take spatula and flip egg after 2.5 minutes
  5. wait another 2.5 minutes until cooked thoroughly
